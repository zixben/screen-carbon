Screen Carbon Test

The Screen Carbon Test measures how well a film or TV show acknowledges and addresses the issue of climate change. It allows you to rate the on-screen depiction of the environment and climate change using a questionnaire, and then adds your score to an aggregated, user-generated Screen Carbon Rating. The test is mainly aimed at evaluating films and TV shows produced since the United Nations' declaration of a Global Climate Emergency in December 2020, but you can use it to rate any production.
