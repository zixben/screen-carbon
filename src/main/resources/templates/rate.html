Introduction to the rating system

To rate its ‘Green’ credentials, all you need to do is to respond to 15 questions about this movie. You will be asked how strongly you ‘Agree’ or ‘Disagree’ with fifteen statements about what you have seen and heard.

Once you have completed the questionnaire simply click ‘Finish’ and the programme will calculate your rating for the film.

‘Your Rating’ will then be shown next to the ‘Average Rating’ of all other contributors.
