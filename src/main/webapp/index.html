The Screen Carbon Test

The Screen Carbon Test seeks to quantify how well a film or TV show acknowledges and addresses the issue of climate change during the Global Climate Emergency. It lets you rate and compare the environmental credentials of screen productions through a user-generated environmental rating questionnaire.
